dob | Livepeer 09/01/2023 13:20
Great questions. The amount of stake is one input into an off chain work selection algorithm. So it is not protocol enforced. Stake represents security, in that it can theoretically be slashed for provably malicious transcoding (though that‚Äôs also not enabled in the protocol yet). Hence broadcasters would prefer to route work towards nodes with more to lose by cheating. Regarding capacity, it actually is more like a right of first refusal. More stake means you‚Äôll be selected to begin performing video transcoding on a stream, but if you can‚Äôt keep up then another node will be selected.
